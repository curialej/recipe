Easy Orange Chicken Recipe

Make the tastiest orange chikcen in under 30 minutes! A healthy and delicious solution to switching up your dinner rountine!

Ingredients and Materials

    ¾Cup of Orange Marmalade
    ¾Cup of Hawaiian BBQ Sauce/Marinade
    2TBSP of Soy Sauce
    Olive Oil
    3 Chicken Breasts
    Crushed Red Pepper (to taste)
    White Rice
    Pot
    Deep Skillet

Cooking Instructions

    1. Place a drop of olive oil into the pan, so that the chicken cooks evenly.
    2. Place chicken (seasoned as desired) into the pan and cook on medium heat, flipping the chicken breast after 2 minutes per side.
    3. Once chicken has started cooking, add in remaining ingredient: orange marmalade, BBQ sauce, and soy sauce.
    4. Let simmer on low to medium heat for 15-20 minutes.
    5. While chicken is cooking, prepare the white rice. Bring 3 cups of water to a boil and add one packet of "boil in bag" rice.
    6. Cook rice 8-10 minutes.
    7. Once chicken has finished cooking, let cool for 5 minutes.
    8. Serve orange chicken over rice, adding crushed red pepper to taste.
